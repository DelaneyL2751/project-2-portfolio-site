Architectural Ring

This project prompt was to choose an architecture style, and then model a ring in that style. One of the stipulations was that the ring must extend off or along the finger at least 3 inches, it couldn't be just a simple band.

I’ve always been a fan of eco-brutalism. The sharp contrast between the lush, organic shapes of nature and harsh, angular lines of brutalist architecture has always appealed to me. One of the stipulations of the assignment was that the ring had to be tall. It couldn’t just be a simple band, so either had to extend off or along the finger in some way. I went for a towered tier look, mimicking the different segments of the finger. This project challenged my rendering skills, as the grass texture was difficult to work with and required extensive rendering time. My only frustration with this is that if you wear to fabricate and wear this ring, you would be unable to bend your finger. But I never claimed it was practical. ;)
