About Me

Hi! I’m Delaney. I’m a graphic design major at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee! I’m also the vice president of UWM’s chapter of AIGA (American Institute of Graphic Arts) so it’s safe to say my life revolves around design. I have experience in the full Adobe suite, Figma, Wordpress and Canva. Outside of design, I always trying new mediums and methods, all while trying to grow into the best artist and person I can be. In my work, both personal and school related, I gravitate towards organic shapes, natural textures and muted colors palettes. Outside of art, I spend a lot of time with my family and friends, who are my best sources of inspiration and support. Thanks for viewing my portfolio!
