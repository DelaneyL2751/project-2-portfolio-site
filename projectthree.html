Movie Poster and Opening Sequence

For this assignment, we were tasked with creating a short film. Then partner up with a classmate, swap films, and produce a poster and title card for their film.

My partner's film was a gritty, murder mystery, noir film. I took inspiration from the silent film era, where often gold and ornate visuals were partnered with grayscale imagery. I liked the contrast between the gold and gray tones, as well as the usage of lines. One of the stipulations of the project was some form of motion in the title sequence. For mine, I utilized After Effects and it's vector compatibility to draw in the lines, a relatively simple effect, but one that took several tutorials and workshopping to get right. As for the posters, I had 3 fairly different ideas going in. After critique from my peers, I ended up combining elements of all three. To this day, I will still often make at least 3 different early iterations of whatever project I'm working on, so I can then synthesize my ideas.
